The Fest

A current strong
enough to move
the whole campus.

Pravah is SKIT's annual one-day fest — a surge of music, art, tech, and talent that pulls every branch and every batch into one current. From the mainstage to the quiet corner where the poster contest happens, the whole campus moves to the same rhythm for a day.

Compete, perform, volunteer, or just show up — however you enter the flow, Pravah is built to carry you somewhere new.
